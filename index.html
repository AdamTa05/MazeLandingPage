Maze Project

A thrilling 3D maze game created using raycasting and SDL2

Play Now

Key Features

Immersive 3D Environment

Experience the maze in a fully immersive 3D environment. Our game uses advanced raycasting techniques to create realistic and engaging visuals, making you feel like you're truly navigating through a complex labyrinth.

Smooth Player Controls

Navigate the maze with ease using our intuitive control system. Whether you're using the keyboard or mouse, the responsive controls ensure smooth movement and rotation, enhancing your gameplay experience.

Dynamic Obstacles and Challenges

Face various challenges as you explore the maze, including moving obstacles and enemies. The dynamic elements keep the gameplay exciting and unpredictable, ensuring a thrilling experience every time you play.
